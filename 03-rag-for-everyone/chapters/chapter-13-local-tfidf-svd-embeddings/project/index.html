Project: Local TF-IDF Search Lab

Build a local retrieval lab that ranks documents, explains matched terms, and projects results into tiny topic dimensions.

starter.pysolution.py

What you will build

  • A local tokenizer and TF-IDF vectorizer.
  • A cosine ranking engine.
  • A tiny topic projection that demonstrates the SVD/latent-topic idea.
  • A retrieval trace with matched terms, missing terms, score, and strategy warning.
  • A production recommendation: local, neural, or hybrid.
Product-level outcome

Transparent retrieval

Your project should show why a document ranked high, what terms matched, what terms were missing, and whether local lexical retrieval is enough for the query.
