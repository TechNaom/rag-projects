# Module 12: Hands-on Projects — Master Chapter

**Outcome:** Turn the earlier modules into a portfolio of RAG builds you can explain, defend, and iterate on publicly.

## Why this module matters

Projects are where the entire course comes together. Each build should force a real design trade-off, not just recreate a tutorial screenshot.

## Example-driven lens

Use one running scenario through the entire module. A policy assistant, support copilot, or internal developer helper works well because it makes the retrieval trade-offs concrete.

## What a strong learner should be able to do

- Explain the topic in plain language
- Show one example
- Name one trade-off
- Defend one design choice
