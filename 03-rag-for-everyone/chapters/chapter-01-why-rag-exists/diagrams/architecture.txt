Northkeep Policy Assistant: RAG Flow

+-------------------+       +---------------------+
| Employee Question | ----> | Retrieve Top Chunks |
+-------------------+       +---------------------+
                                  |
                                  v
                         +------------------+
                         | Trusted Context  |
                         | policy excerpts  |
                         +------------------+
                                  |
                                  v
                         +------------------+
                         | Grounded Prompt  |
                         | rules + context  |
                         +------------------+
                                  |
                                  v
                         +------------------+
                         | Generated Answer |
                         | cites sources    |
                         +------------------+
                                  |
                                  v
                         +------------------+
                         | Evaluation       |
                         | Recall@K + MRR   |
                         +------------------+

Key idea:
The model does not answer first. The system retrieves evidence first.

Failure points to inspect:
- Wrong document retrieved.
- Useful context split across chunks.
- Prompt does not force citation.
- Answer contains facts not found in context.
- Evaluation misses an important real-world question.
