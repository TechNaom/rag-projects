Interview Questions: Why RAG Exists

What problem does RAG solve?

Strong answer: RAG lets an AI system answer from trusted external context instead of relying only on model memory.

Red flag: "RAG just makes the model smarter."

Follow-up: When would long context be enough?

Why are citations important?

Strong answer: Citations let users verify that an answer came from approved policy sources.

Red flag: "The model is usually right."

Follow-up: What should the assistant do when context is insufficient?

Why can a good prompt still produce a bad RAG answer?

Strong answer: Bad retrieval gives the prompt irrelevant or missing evidence.

Red flag: "Just tell the model not to hallucinate."

Follow-up: Which retrieval metrics would you use?
