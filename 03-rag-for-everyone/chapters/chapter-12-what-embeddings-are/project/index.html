Project: Tiny Embedding Search Lab

Build a dependency-free search lab that ranks tiny vectors and reports production warnings.

starter.pysolution.py

What you will build

  • A cosine similarity function.
  • A tiny in-memory vector index.
  • A top-k retrieval function.
  • A metadata safety warning for stale and restricted chunks.
  • A retrieval report that explains why a chunk was selected.
Product-level outcome

Search by meaning, inspect with discipline

The project should show learners both sides: semantic search is useful, and production retrieval still needs metadata, warnings, and explainable reports.
