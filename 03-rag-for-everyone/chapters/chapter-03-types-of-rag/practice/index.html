Practice Bank: Types of RAG

Choose the architecture pattern for each scenario. The best answer is not always the most advanced answer.

Scenario 1

Internal FAQ

One clean handbook, low-risk Q&A.

Scenario 2

Refund Assistant

Country, tier, and policy version affect the answer.

Scenario 3

Security Runbook

Weak retrieval must trigger retry, refusal, or escalation.

Scenario 4

Contract Relationships

Answer depends on clauses, customers, products, and obligations.

Scenario 5

Support Ticket Agent

Assistant retrieves docs, asks follow-up, and opens a ticket after approval.

Scenario 6

Multimodal Product Knowledge

Answers require slides, screenshots, tables, and text docs.

1 Production selection task

Pick six scenarios. Choose RAG type, risk, eval, observability signal, and why simpler options fail or succeed.
