# Blog Post Draft — Embeddings

Embeddings is one of the places where RAG stops being a demo and starts becoming a system. In this module, we break the topic into small decisions, concrete examples, and interview-ready trade-offs. By the end, the reader should know not only what each concept means, but when they would actually use it.

## What we cover

- What embeddings are
- Vector spaces
- Similarity
- Dimensionality
- Distance metrics
- Cosine similarity
- Euclidean distance
- Dot product
- Embedding models
- Domain-specific embeddings
- Fine-tuning
- Embedding drift
- Re-indexing
- Provider comparisons: OpenAI, Cohere, Google, and BAAI
