# Module 4: Embeddings — Master Chapter

**Outcome:** Explain vector similarity, compare embedding models, and reason about drift, dimensionality, and re-indexing.

## Why this module matters

Embeddings convert meaning into numbers so search can happen at scale. Once you understand the geometry, retrieval design becomes far less mysterious.

## Subtopics

- What embeddings are
- Vector spaces
- Similarity
- Dimensionality
- Distance metrics
- Cosine similarity
- Euclidean distance
- Dot product
- Embedding models
- Domain-specific embeddings
- Fine-tuning
- Embedding drift
- Re-indexing
- Provider comparisons: OpenAI, Cohere, Google, and BAAI

## Example-driven lens

Use one running scenario through the entire module. A policy assistant, support copilot, or internal developer helper works well because it makes the retrieval trade-offs concrete.

## What a strong learner should be able to do

- Explain the topic in plain language
- Show one example
- Name one trade-off
- Defend one design choice
