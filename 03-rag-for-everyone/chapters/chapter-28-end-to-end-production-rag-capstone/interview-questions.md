# Interview Questions: Production RAG Capstone

1. What makes a RAG capstone production-grade?
2. How do you choose a capstone track?
3. What makes a product worth revisiting?
4. How do you keep the MVP finishable?
5. What belongs in the final README?
6. What should the architecture diagram prove?
7. What should the eval report contain?
8. How do you demo failure handling without making the product look broken?
9. What would you harden first after the MVP?
10. What is the senior engineering answer?

Senior answer: choose a real repeated workflow, build the smallest end-to-end system that proves it, then show architecture, evals, traces, costs, failure analysis, deployment trade-offs, and a roadmap that makes the next increment obvious.
