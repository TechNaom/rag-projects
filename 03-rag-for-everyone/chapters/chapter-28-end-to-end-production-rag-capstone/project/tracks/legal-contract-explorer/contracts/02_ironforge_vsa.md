# Contract: Ironforge Logistics LLC — Vendor Services Agreement

## 1. Term and Termination
The initial term of this Agreement is thirty-six (36) months from the Effective Date and renews automatically for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party gives ninety (90) days written notice of non-renewal. Either party may terminate for convenience with ninety (90) days prior written notice. For material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate with immediate effect if the breach is not cured within forty-five (45) days of written notice.

## 2. Limitation of Liability
The aggregate liability of Ironforge Logistics under this Agreement is capped at five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000), regardless of the form of action. Neither party shall be liable for any special, indirect, or consequential damages. This limitation does not apply to a party's breach of its confidentiality obligations or to damages caused by fraud.

## 3. Indemnification
Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other party against third-party claims for bodily injury, death, or physical damage to tangible property to the extent caused by the indemnifying party's negligent acts or omissions. This mutual indemnification obligation survives the termination of this Agreement.

## 4. Payment Terms
All invoices are due and payable within net forty-five (45) days from receipt. Ironforge Logistics offers a two percent (2%) early-payment discount for invoices settled within ten (10) days. Disputed amounts must be raised in writing within fifteen (15) days of the invoice date, and the parties shall negotiate such disputes in good faith.

## 5. Confidentiality
Confidential Information disclosed under this Agreement shall be used solely for the purpose of performing the Services. The receiving party shall limit access to Confidential Information to employees and contractors with a need to know who are bound by written obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement. These confidentiality obligations continue in perpetuity for trade secrets and for five (5) years for all other Confidential Information.

## 6. Service Levels
Ironforge Logistics shall meet a monthly on-time delivery rate of at least ninety-eight percent (98%). Failure to meet this service level for two consecutive months entitles the Client to service credits equal to five percent (5%) of the monthly fee for each month the service level is missed.
