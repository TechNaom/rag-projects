# Contract: Meridian Facilities Group — Facilities Management Agreement

## 1. Term and Termination
This Agreement runs for an initial period of eighteen (18) months and continues month-to-month thereafter. Either party may terminate for convenience by giving thirty (30) days written notice once the initial period has ended. Meridian Facilities Group may terminate immediately for cause if the Client fails to provide safe site access or fails to pay undisputed amounts for more than sixty (60) days after the due date.

## 3. Indemnification
Meridian Facilities Group shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Client and its representatives from and against all third-party claims, losses, and expenses, including reasonable attorney fees, arising from the negligence or willful misconduct of Meridian's personnel while performing on-site Services. The Client shall promptly notify Meridian of any such claim and grant Meridian sole control over its defense and settlement.

## 4. Payment Terms
Client shall pay monthly service fees within net thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Meridian Facilities Group reserves the right to adjust fees annually by an amount not to exceed the change in the Consumer Price Index over the preceding twelve (12) months, upon sixty (60) days written notice. Pass-through costs for materials and subcontractors are billed at cost plus ten percent (10%).

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party shall treat the other party's business, financial, and operational information as confidential and shall not use it except as necessary to perform under this Agreement. These obligations survive for two (2) years after termination. Building access codes, security procedures, and floor plans shall be treated as Confidential Information indefinitely.

## 6. Insurance
Meridian Facilities Group shall maintain commercial general insurance coverage of at least two million dollars ($2,000,000) per occurrence and shall name the Client as an additional insured. Certificates of insurance shall be provided to the Client upon request and prior to commencing any on-site work.
