# Contract: Quillfeather Media Co. — Marketing Services Agreement

## 1. Term and Termination
This Agreement is effective for a campaign term of six (6) months. Either party may terminate for convenience with fifteen (15) days prior written notice, provided that the Client remains responsible for any media commitments Quillfeather has already placed on the Client's behalf. Either party may terminate immediately for cause upon a material breach that remains uncured for ten (10) days after written notice.

## 2. Limitation of Liability
Quillfeather Media's total liability for any claim arising under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid for the specific campaign giving rise to the claim during the three (3) months preceding the claim. Quillfeather is not responsible for the performance results of any advertising campaign, including impressions, clicks, or conversions, which depend on factors outside Quillfeather's control.

## 3. Indemnification
Quillfeather Media shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client from third-party claims that creative materials produced by Quillfeather infringe a copyright or trademark. The Client shall indemnify Quillfeather against claims arising from Client-supplied content, product claims, or materials that Quillfeather incorporates at the Client's written direction.

## 4. Payment Terms
Client shall pay a fifty percent (50%) deposit upon signing and the remaining balance within net fifteen (15) days of campaign completion. Third-party media spend is billed in advance and is non-refundable once placed. Overdue balances accrue interest at two percent (2%) per month and may result in the suspension of active campaigns.

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party shall keep confidential the other party's marketing strategies, customer lists, and unpublished campaign materials. Confidentiality obligations survive for eighteen (18) months after termination. Quillfeather may, with the Client's prior written approval, reference the Client's name and non-confidential campaign results in Quillfeather's own promotional portfolio.

## 6. Intellectual Property
Upon full payment, the Client owns all final creative deliverables produced specifically for the Client under this Agreement. Quillfeather retains ownership of its pre-existing tools, templates, and internal know-how, and grants the Client a non-exclusive license to use them as embedded in the deliverables.
