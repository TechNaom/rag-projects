# Contract: Cloudspire Systems Ltd. — SaaS Subscription Agreement

## 1. Term and Termination
The subscription term is twelve (12) months and renews automatically for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides thirty (30) days written notice before the end of the current term. Cloudspire Systems may suspend or terminate access immediately if Client's use of the platform threatens the security or integrity of the service. There is no termination for convenience during a paid subscription term; fees for the current term are non-refundable.

## 2. Limitation of Liability
Cloudspire Systems' total liability arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Client in the six (6) months immediately preceding the claim. The platform is provided on an "as is" basis and Cloudspire disclaims all implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose to the maximum extent permitted by law. Neither party is liable for consequential or incidental damages.

## 4. Payment Terms
Subscription fees are billed annually in advance and are due within net fifteen (15) days of the invoice date. All fees are non-cancelable and non-refundable except as expressly stated. If payment is not received within fifteen (15) days of the due date, Cloudspire Systems may suspend the Client's access to the platform until the outstanding balance is paid in full.

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party shall keep confidential all non-public information disclosed by the other party and shall not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent. The receiving party's confidentiality obligations remain in effect for two (2) years following the expiration or termination of this Agreement. Aggregate and anonymized usage statistics that do not identify the Client are not considered Confidential Information.

## 6. Data Protection
Cloudspire Systems processes Client personal data only in accordance with the Client's documented instructions and maintains appropriate technical and organizational security measures. Cloudspire shall notify the Client without undue delay, and in any case within seventy-two (72) hours, after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting Client data.
