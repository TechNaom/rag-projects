# Inviting and Managing Team Members

On the Team plan you can share notebooks with coworkers and control what each person can do. This article explains invites, roles, and removing members.

## Invite someone to your workspace

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter their email address and pick a role: **Admin**, **Editor**, or **Viewer**.
4. Click **Send invite**. They get an email with a join link that expires after 14 days.

A pending invite shows up in the Members list as "Invited". You can resend or revoke it before they accept.

## What each role can do

- **Admin** manages billing, invites and removes members, and edits any notebook.
- **Editor** creates and edits notebooks they have been given access to, but cannot manage members or billing.
- **Viewer** reads and comments on shared notebooks, but cannot edit content.

Roles are set per workspace. You can change someone's role later from the Members list by clicking their name and choosing a new role.

## Remove a member

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click the member you want to remove.
3. Click **Remove from workspace** and confirm.

Notebooks the removed person created stay in the workspace and are reassigned to an Admin. The removed person immediately loses access on all their devices.
