# Resetting Your Notedeck Password

Notedeck is a note-taking and task app. This article explains how to reset or change your account password.

## Reset a forgotten password

If you cannot sign in because you forgot your password:

1. Go to the Notedeck sign-in page and click **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter the email address on your account and click **Send reset link**.
3. Open the email titled "Reset your Notedeck password" and click the button inside. The link expires after 60 minutes.
4. Enter a new password. It must be at least 10 characters and include one number.
5. Click **Save password**. You are signed out of all devices and must sign in again with the new password.

If the reset email does not arrive within 5 minutes, check your spam folder and confirm you typed the same email you signed up with. Notedeck only sends reset links to addresses that already have an account.

## Change your password while signed in

If you know your current password and just want to change it:

1. Open **Settings > Account > Security**.
2. Click **Change password**.
3. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
4. Click **Update**. Other devices stay signed in unless you choose **Sign out everywhere**.

## Password reset link expired or invalid

Reset links are single-use and expire after 60 minutes. If you see "This link is no longer valid", request a fresh link from **Forgot password?** and use the newest email. Older reset emails stop working as soon as a newer one is sent.
