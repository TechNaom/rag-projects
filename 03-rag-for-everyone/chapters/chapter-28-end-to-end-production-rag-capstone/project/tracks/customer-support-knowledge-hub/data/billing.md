# Billing and Subscription Changes

This article covers upgrading, downgrading, and cancelling your Notedeck subscription, plus how refunds and invoices work.

## Upgrade or downgrade your plan

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. Choose **Free**, **Pro**, or **Team** and click **Confirm**.

Upgrades take effect immediately and you are charged a prorated amount for the rest of the current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current period, so you keep paid features until then.

## Cancel your subscription

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription** at the bottom of the page.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

Your account stays on the paid plan until the end of the period you already paid for, then drops to Free. Cancelling does not delete your data. Notebooks beyond the Free limit become read-only rather than being erased.

## Update your payment method

Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment method** and click **Update card**. Notedeck charges the new card on the next billing date. If a charge fails, we retry for 7 days and email you before any plan change.

## Refunds and invoices

Every charge generates an invoice you can download from **Settings > Billing > Invoices**. Notedeck offers a full refund if you cancel within 14 days of a new annual charge. Monthly plans are not refundable, but you can cancel any time to stop future charges.
