# Exporting Your Notes and Tasks

You can export your Notedeck data at any time. Exports are useful for backups, for moving to another tool, or for keeping an offline copy.

## Export a single notebook

1. Open the notebook you want to export.
2. Click the **...** menu in the top right and choose **Export notebook**.
3. Pick a format: **Markdown (.md)**, **PDF**, or **HTML**.
4. Click **Export**. The file downloads to your browser's download folder.

Markdown keeps your headings, checklists, and links as plain text. PDF and HTML preserve layout but are harder to edit later.

## Export your entire account

To download everything at once:

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Data & privacy**.
2. Click **Request full export**.
3. Notedeck builds a single ZIP archive of every notebook, task list, and attachment. This can take up to 24 hours for large accounts.
4. When it is ready, you get an email with a download link. The link stays active for 7 days.

The full export contains one Markdown file per note plus an `attachments/` folder. Tasks are exported as a `tasks.csv` file with columns for title, due date, status, and notebook.

## What is not included in an export

Exports include your notes, tasks, and attachments. They do not include comments from other team members, version history, or trashed items. Empty the trash before exporting if you want a clean archive.
