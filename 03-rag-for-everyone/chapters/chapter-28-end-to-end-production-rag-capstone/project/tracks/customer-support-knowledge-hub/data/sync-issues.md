# Troubleshooting Sync Issues

Notedeck syncs your notes across every device you sign in on. If a change on one device is not showing up on another, work through the steps below.

## Notes are not syncing between devices

1. Confirm every device is signed in to the **same** Notedeck account. A second account is the most common cause of "missing" notes.
2. Check the sync indicator in the bottom left. A spinning icon means a sync is in progress; a red dot means sync is paused.
3. Make sure the device is online. Notedeck queues changes made offline and syncs them when the connection returns.
4. Pull to refresh (mobile) or press **Ctrl/Cmd + R** (desktop) to force a sync.

## Sync is stuck or paused

If the sync indicator shows a red dot for more than a few minutes:

1. Open **Settings > Sync** and check the status message.
2. Click **Pause sync** and then **Resume sync** to restart the connection.
3. If it stays stuck, sign out and back in on that one device. Your notes are safe on the server and re-download after sign-in.

## A sync conflict happened

If the same note was edited on two devices while one was offline, Notedeck keeps both versions rather than overwriting your work. The note shows a **Conflicted copy** banner. Open the banner to compare the two versions side by side and pick which text to keep, or merge them manually. Delete the copy you no longer need once you are done.
