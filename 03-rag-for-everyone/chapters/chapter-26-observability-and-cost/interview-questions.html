Interview Questions: Observability and Cost

  1. What belongs in a RAG trace?
  2. Why are aggregate dashboards not enough?
  3. How do you attribute token cost to product features?
  4. What operational signals reveal retrieval problems?
  5. How would you investigate a slow answer?
  6. What cost levers exist before changing providers?
  7. How should cache hit rate influence architecture?
  8. What quality metrics must be watched before cost optimization?
  9. How do you design a release gate for cost and quality?
  10. What is the senior engineering answer?

Senior answer

Instrument every stage, slice by route and tenant, compare against baselines, and optimize spend only when groundedness and supportability remain intact.
