Exercises: Build Parser Quality Gates

Use starter.py to classify parsed documents, detect cleaning risk, and decide whether content can move to chunking.

starter.pysolution.py

1 Detect parser readiness

Return ready, ready_with_warnings, needs_review, or rejected based on quality signals.

2 Preserve required metadata

Validate source id, format, version, access label, owner, and source positions.

3 Explain the first production risk

Identify whether the top risk is OCR, table extraction, boilerplate noise, missing metadata, or security metadata loss.

4 Recommend the next action

Decide whether to publish to chunking, route to review, fix parser config, or reject the source.
