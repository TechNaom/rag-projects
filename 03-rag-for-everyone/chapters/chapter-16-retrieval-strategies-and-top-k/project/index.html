Project: Top-K Retrieval Policy Planner

Build a planner that chooses retrieval policy from query intent, risk, score distribution, and trace signals.

starter.pysolution.py

What you will build

  • A query-intent to top-k policy mapper.
  • A retrieval miss diagnostic engine.
  • A controls recommender for filters, rewrite, multi-query, dedupe, and reranking.
  • A trace report explaining the retrieval decision.
Product-level outcome

Evidence selection policy

Your project should output a practical retrieval policy: "For high-risk policy exceptions use k=10, EU active filters, query rewrite, parent expansion, dedupe, and reranking."
