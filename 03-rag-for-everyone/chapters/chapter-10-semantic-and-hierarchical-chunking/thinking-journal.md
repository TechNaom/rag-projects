# GenAI Thought Process Journal: Semantic and Hierarchical Chunking

## Builder Reflection

Before adding advanced chunking, I will ask:

1. Did recursive chunking fail real golden questions?
2. Are topic boundaries unstable?
3. Does small evidence need parent context?
4. Can I preserve parent-child ids?
5. Can I enforce access on expanded context?
6. Can I evaluate child retrieval and parent expansion separately?

## Production Mindset

Advanced chunking should earn its place. Complexity is useful only when it improves evidence quality, citation trust, or answer faithfulness.
