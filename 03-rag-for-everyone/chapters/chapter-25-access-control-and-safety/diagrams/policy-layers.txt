Layer                    Purpose
----------------------   -------------------------------------------------------
Authentication           Know which identity made the request
Authorization metadata   Know roles, tenant, department, groups, and expiration
Pre-filtering            Keep unauthorized documents out of candidate retrieval
Post-filtering           Catch stale metadata, mixed chunks, and policy drift
Prompt boundary          Treat retrieved text as data, never instructions
Refusal policy           Decline unsafe or unauthorized requests without leakage
Audit logging            Preserve decisions for review, compliance, and response
Replay tests             Prove access and safety behavior survives future changes
