# GenAI Thought Process Journal: Fixed and Recursive Chunking

## Builder Reflection

Before blaming retrieval, I will inspect the chunks:

1. Is the chunk too small?
2. Is the chunk too large?
3. Did the split break a rule from its exception?
4. Did overlap create duplicates?
5. Does the chunk carry source metadata?
6. Do golden questions retrieve the right chunks?

## Production Mindset

Chunking is not a preprocessing detail. It is evidence design.
