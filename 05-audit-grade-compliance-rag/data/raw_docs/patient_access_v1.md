# Patient Data Access Control Policy

**Policy ID:** PACC-001
**Version:** 1.0 (SUPERSEDED)
**Owner:** Information Security / Privacy Office
**Applies to:** All workforce members with access to electronic PHI

## 1. Purpose
This policy governs who may access patient protected health information (PHI) within Aventine Health Network systems, in compliance with the HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules.

## 2. Minimum Necessary Standard
Workforce members may access only the minimum PHI necessary to perform their assigned duties. Access to records outside a member's assigned patient panel or department requires documented justification.

## 3. Access Request Process
New access requests are submitted to the member's supervisor and approved by the department manager. Access is granted within five (5) business days of approval.

## 4. Patient Right of Access
Patients may request a copy of their own medical record. AHN must provide the requested records within **thirty (30) days** of a completed written request.

## 5. Access Reviews
Supervisors review the access rights of their direct reports annually to confirm continued business need.

## 6. Termination of Access
When a workforce member changes roles or leaves AHN, their PHI access must be revoked within 72 hours.
