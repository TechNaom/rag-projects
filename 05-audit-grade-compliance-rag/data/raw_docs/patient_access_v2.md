# Patient Data Access Control Policy

**Policy ID:** PACC-002
**Version:** 2.0 (CURRENT)
**Owner:** Information Security / Privacy Office
**Applies to:** All workforce members with access to electronic PHI

## 1. Purpose
This policy governs who may access patient protected health information (PHI) within Aventine Health Network systems, in compliance with the HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules. Version 2.0 supersedes PACC-001 and introduces role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication requirements.

## 2. Minimum Necessary Standard
Workforce members may access only the minimum PHI necessary to perform their assigned duties. Under this version, access is enforced through role-based access control (RBAC) profiles rather than individually granted permissions.

## 3. Access Request Process
New access requests are submitted through the Identity Governance portal and approved by both the department manager and the Privacy Office. Access is granted within three (3) business days of full approval. All privileged access now requires multi-factor authentication (MFA).

## 4. Patient Right of Access
Patients may request a copy of their own medical record. AHN must provide the requested records within **fifteen (15) days** of a completed written request, reduced from the previous thirty-day standard.

## 5. Access Reviews
Supervisors and the Privacy Office jointly review the access rights of all workforce members semi-annually to confirm continued business need.

## 6. Termination of Access
When a workforce member changes roles or leaves AHN, their PHI access must be revoked within 24 hours through automated deprovisioning.
