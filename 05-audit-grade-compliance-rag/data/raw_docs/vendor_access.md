# Third-Party Vendor Access Policy

**Policy ID:** VEND-001
**Version:** 1.0 (CURRENT)
**Owner:** Vendor Risk Management / Privacy Office
**Applies to:** All third-party vendors and their AHN sponsors

## 1. Purpose
This policy governs how third-party vendors and business associates are granted access to Aventine Health Network systems and protected health information (PHI).

## 2. Business Associate Agreements
No vendor may access PHI until a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is in place. The BAA must define permitted uses of PHI, security safeguards, and breach notification obligations.

## 3. Security Assessment
Every vendor with PHI access must complete a security risk assessment prior to onboarding and re-assessment at least once every twelve (12) months.

## 4. Least-Privilege Access
Vendors are granted the minimum system access necessary for their contracted services. Vendor accounts must be uniquely attributable to a named individual and must not be shared.

## 5. Access Expiration
Vendor access is time-bound and automatically expires at the end of the contract term unless formally renewed. Dormant vendor accounts inactive for 60 days are automatically disabled.

## 6. Offboarding
Upon contract termination, the sponsoring department must confirm all vendor access has been revoked within 24 hours and that any AHN data held by the vendor is returned or destroyed per the BAA.
