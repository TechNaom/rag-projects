# Northkeep National Bank — Health & Wellness Benefits Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-BEN-005
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Human Resources — Total Rewards
**Applies to:** All benefits-eligible employees (regularly scheduled 20+ hours/week)

## 1. Medical Plan Options
Northkeep offers three medical plan tiers through Sentinel Health Partners:
- **Core PPO** — Bank pays 80% of premium; $1,500 individual / $3,000 family deductible
- **Plus PPO** — Bank pays 75% of premium; $750 individual / $1,500 family deductible
- **High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) + HSA** — Bank pays 90% of premium; $3,000 individual / $6,000 family deductible; Bank contributes $750 (individual) or $1,500 (family) annually to employee's HSA

New hires must enroll within 30 days of their start date or wait until the next Open Enrollment period (typically November).

## 2. Dental and Vision
Dental coverage (Core and Enhanced tiers) and vision coverage are offered as voluntary benefits at employee cost, with Bank subsidy of 50% of the base Core Dental premium.

## 3. 401(k) Retirement Plan
Northkeep matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of eligible pay, plus 50% of the next 2% (effectively up to 5% match on a 6% contribution). Employees are immediately vested in their own contributions; Bank matching contributions vest 20% per year of service, fully vested after 5 years.

## 4. Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)
Employees may contribute up to 10% of base salary to purchase Northkeep stock at a 15% discount off the lower of the price at the start or end of each 6-month offering period, subject to a $25,000 annual IRS limit.

## 5. Mental Health and EAP
All employees and household members have access to the Employee Assistance Program (EAP), offering 8 free confidential counseling sessions per issue per year, available 24/7 by phone or virtual visit. Mental health visits under the medical plan are covered at the same cost-share as any other specialist visit (no separate higher copay).

## 6. Wellness Reimbursement
Employees may submit up to $500 per year in wellness reimbursements covering gym memberships, fitness equipment, or wellness apps, submitted via the Benefits Portal with receipts.

## 7. Tuition Assistance
Northkeep reimburses up to $5,250 per calendar year (the current IRS tax-free maximum) for job-related coursework or a degree program, with a grade requirement of B or above (or Pass for pass/fail courses), and a 1-year service commitment after reimbursement or the amount must be repaid pro-rata.

## 8. Dependent Care FSA
Employees may contribute up to $5,000 pre-tax annually to a Dependent Care Flexible Spending Account for eligible childcare or elder care expenses.
