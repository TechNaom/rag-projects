# Northkeep National Bank — Annual Compliance Training Requirements

**Policy ID:** COMP-TRN-009
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Corporate Compliance / Learning & Development
**Applies to:** All employees; additional modules for specific roles as noted

## 1. Purpose
Federal and state banking regulators require Northkeep to ensure employees understand the laws and internal policies relevant to their roles. This policy sets the mandatory training calendar.

## 2. Core Annual Modules (All Employees)
Every employee must complete the following modules by December 15 each year, assigned and tracked via the LearnNorthkeep platform:
- Code of Conduct Refresher (Policy HR-COC-001) — 20 minutes
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) Basics — 30 minutes
- Data Privacy & Customer Confidentiality (Policy COMP-DP-008) — 25 minutes
- Conflicts of Interest & Insider Trading (Policy COMP-COI-002) — 20 minutes
- Anti-Harassment & Equal Opportunity (Policy HR-EEO-004) — 25 minutes
- Information Security Awareness (phishing, password hygiene) — 20 minutes

## 3. Role-Specific Modules
In addition to core training, the following roles require additional annual modules:
- **Branch & Teller staff:** Robbery & Physical Security Response; Elder Financial Abuse Detection
- **Lending & Underwriting:** Fair Lending (ECOA, Fair Housing Act); Flood Insurance Compliance
- **Investment Banking & Markets:** Market Conduct & Information Barriers; Personal Trading Pre-Clearance procedures
- **Wealth Management & Advisory:** Suitability & Fiduciary Duty (Regulation Best Interest)
- **Managers (all departments):** Workplace Investigations 101; Performance Management & Documentation

## 4. New Hire Requirements
New employees must complete all applicable core modules within 30 days of their start date, and role-specific modules within 60 days. Access to certain systems (e.g., core banking platform, wire transfer systems) is automatically suspended if required training is not completed by the deadline.

## 5. Tracking and Escalation
Completion is tracked centrally by Compliance. Employees with incomplete mandatory training receive automated reminders at 14 days, 7 days, and 1 day before the deadline. After the deadline, the employee's manager and the employee's department Compliance Officer are notified, and repeated non-completion (more than 2 modules missed in a calendar year) is escalated to HR for inclusion in the employee's performance review.

## 6. Assessment and Passing Score
Each module includes a knowledge check; employees must score 80% or higher to receive credit. Employees who do not pass may retake the assessment up to 3 times before the module is escalated to a live session with their Compliance Officer.

## 7. Recordkeeping
Training records are retained for 7 years in accordance with regulatory examination requirements and may be requested by the OCC, CFPB, or state regulators during an examination.
