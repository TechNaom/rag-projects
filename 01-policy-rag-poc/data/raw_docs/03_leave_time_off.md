# Northkeep National Bank — Leave & Time-Off Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-LV-003
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Human Resources
**Applies to:** All full-time and part-time benefits-eligible employees

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue PTO based on tenure:
- 0–2 years of service: 15 days/year (1.25 days/month)
- 3–5 years of service: 20 days/year (1.67 days/month)
- 6–10 years of service: 25 days/year (2.08 days/month)
- 11+ years of service: 28 days/year (2.33 days/month)

Part-time employees (20–34 hours/week) accrue PTO on a pro-rated basis. PTO may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 unused days into the following calendar year; any balance above this is forfeited unless local law requires otherwise.

## 2. Sick Leave
All employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year, separate from PTO, available immediately upon hire (not accrued). Sick leave may be used for the employee's own illness or to care for an immediate family member. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## 3. Parental Leave
Northkeep provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for the primary caregiver and 8 weeks for the secondary caregiver, available to birth parents, adoptive parents, and foster parents, regardless of gender. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or placement and may be split into two blocks with manager approval.

## 4. Bereavement Leave
Employees receive up to 5 paid days for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling) and up to 2 paid days for extended family.

## 5. Jury Duty and Civic Leave
Employees are granted paid leave for jury duty for the full duration of service, and up to 1 paid day per election cycle to vote if their work schedule does not otherwise allow it.

## 6. Unpaid Leave of Absence
Employees with at least 1 year of tenure may request an unpaid personal leave of absence of up to 12 weeks, subject to manager and HR approval, for circumstances not covered by other leave categories. Job protection during unpaid leave follows applicable federal and state law (e.g., FMLA where eligible).

## 7. Requesting Leave
PTO and sick leave requests are submitted through the WorkdayHR self-service portal. PTO requests of 3 or more consecutive days require manager approval at least 2 weeks in advance except in emergencies. Sick leave may be reported the same day, before the start of the employee's shift, via the portal or by notifying their manager directly.

## 8. Holidays
Northkeep observes 11 paid federal holidays annually. Employees in customer-facing branch roles required to work a holiday receive holiday pay at 1.5x base rate plus a compensatory day off within 30 days.
