# Northkeep National Bank — Social Media & External Communications Policy

**Policy ID:** COMP-SM-011
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Corporate Compliance / Marketing
**Applies to:** All employees

## 1. Purpose
As a regulated financial institution, Northkeep's public communications — including employee social media activity that references the Bank — are subject to regulatory requirements (FINRA, SEC) and reputational risk considerations beyond those of a typical employer.

## 2. Personal Social Media Use
Employees may identify themselves as Northkeep employees on personal social media. When doing so, employees must include a disclaimer that views expressed are their own and not those of the Bank. Employees may not post confidential, proprietary, or customer information under any circumstances, and may not post photos taken inside secure areas (e.g., the vault, data centers, trading floors).

## 3. Discussing the Bank's Products or Stock
Employees other than designated Investor Relations and Communications staff may not make public statements (including social media posts) about Northkeep's financial performance, stock price, or merger/acquisition activity. Employees in Wealth Management, Investment Banking, and Markets are additionally prohibited from discussing specific securities, investment recommendations, or market predictions on any public or semi-public platform (including LinkedIn, X, and Reddit) due to FINRA Rule 2210 communication-with-the-public requirements — such content requires pre-approval from Compliance Marketing Review.

## 4. Responding to Customer Complaints Online
Employees who see a customer complaint about Northkeep on social media should not respond directly, even to defend the Bank. The post should be forwarded to the Social Media Listening team (socialmedia@northkeepnational-internal.example) for an approved, on-brand response.

## 5. Media Inquiries
All media inquiries — including from bloggers, podcasters, and trade publications — must be directed to Corporate Communications. Employees are not authorized to speak to media on the Bank's behalf without explicit prior approval, regardless of seniority.

## 6. Recruiting and Referrals
Employees are encouraged to share approved job postings and Bank news on personal social media (e.g., reposting from the official Northkeep LinkedIn page) without requiring separate pre-approval, as this falls outside the restrictions in Section 3.

## 7. Violations
Unauthorized public statements about the Bank's financial condition, securities, or confidential matters may violate securities law in addition to this policy and can result in termination and regulatory referral, independent of the employee's intent.
