# Northkeep National Bank — Expense & Travel Policy

**Policy ID:** FIN-EXP-006
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Finance — Corporate Travel & Expense
**Applies to:** All employees who incur business expenses

## 1. General Principle
Employees should spend Bank funds as carefully as they would their own. All expenses must be reasonable, business-necessary, and properly documented with itemized receipts for any single expense over $25.

## 2. Air Travel
Domestic flights under 6 hours must be booked in economy class through the Concur travel portal. Flights over 6 hours, or international travel, may be booked in premium economy. Business class requires VP-level approval in advance. Employees should book at least 14 days ahead when travel is foreseeable to control cost.

## 3. Hotel
Hotel bookings should not exceed the GSA per-diem rate for the destination city by more than 20% without prior manager approval. Employees should use preferred corporate hotel partners (Marriott, Hilton corporate rates) where available.

## 4. Meals
Daily meal reimbursement caps (when not on a Bank-sponsored per diem):
- Breakfast: $25
- Lunch: $35
- Dinner: $60
Alcohol is reimbursable only when entertaining a client, up to $50 per person, and must be itemized separately with the business purpose and attendee names noted.

## 5. Ground Transportation
Rideshare and taxi are reimbursable for business travel. Personal vehicle use is reimbursed at the current IRS standard mileage rate (67 cents/mile for 2026). Parking and tolls are reimbursable with receipt.

## 6. Client Entertainment
Client entertainment expenses up to $150 per person require only manager approval; above $150 per person requires Director-level approval and must be logged with the client's name, company, and business purpose for Compliance review (gifts/entertainment to customers are also subject to Policy HR-COC-001 Section 6).

## 7. Submission Deadline
Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred via Concur. Reports submitted more than 90 days late may be denied at Finance's discretion absent extenuating circumstances.

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Items
The Bank does not reimburse: traffic or parking fines, personal entertainment, spa/minibar charges, airline lounge memberships (unless pre-approved for VP+ frequent travelers), or first-class upgrades purchased with personal funds for reimbursement.

## 9. Corporate Card
Employees who travel more than 4 times per year are issued a Northkeep Corporate Travel Card. The card must be used for all eligible travel expenses where accepted; personal charges on the corporate card are prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
