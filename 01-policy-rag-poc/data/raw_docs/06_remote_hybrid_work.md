# Northkeep National Bank — Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-RW-007
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Human Resources
**Applies to:** All employees in eligible roles

## 1. Eligibility
Roles are classified into three categories by the employee's manager and HR Business Partner:
- **On-site Required** — branch staff, vault operations, facilities, and certain Operations roles requiring physical presence
- **Hybrid** — most corporate, technology, and back-office roles; minimum 3 days/week in assigned office
- **Remote-Eligible** — select individual-contributor roles in Technology and certain national/specialist functions, approved case-by-case by VP and above

## 2. Hybrid Schedule Requirements
Hybrid employees must badge into their assigned office a minimum of 3 days per week, including at least one of the team's designated \"anchor days\" set by their manager. Schedule exceptions require manager approval.

## 3. Information Security for Remote Work
Employees working remotely must:
- Connect to Bank systems only via the Northkeep VPN with multi-factor authentication
- Never access customer data (NPI) on personal, unmanaged devices
- Position screens so confidential information is not visible to others (the \"clean screen\" rule applies at home as in the office)
- Store any printed Bank documents securely and shred them when no longer needed; printing of customer NPI at home is prohibited

## 4. Home Office Stipend
Remote-eligible and hybrid employees receive a one-time $500 home office setup stipend and $50/month toward internet and phone costs, processed through payroll.

## 5. Working From Outside the Country
Employees must obtain prior approval from HR and Legal before working from outside their home country for any period exceeding 10 consecutive business days, due to tax, regulatory, and data residency implications specific to banking.

## 6. Performance Expectations
Remote and hybrid work is a privilege tied to sustained performance. Managers may revoke remote/hybrid eligibility for an individual employee in cases of performance concerns or documented misuse, following standard HR consultation.

## 7. Equipment
The Bank provides a laptop, monitor, and headset for all remote and hybrid employees. Personal devices may not be used to access core banking systems under any circumstances.
