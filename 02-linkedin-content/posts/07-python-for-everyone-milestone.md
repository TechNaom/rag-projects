I've been quietly building something every single day. Today felt like the right day to show it. 🐍

Here's what "done" actually means here — not vibes, real numbers:

📘 **11 chapters live**, from `print("Hello, world!")` all the way to closures, decorators, and importing your first modules

💼 **Interview-question banks** on every chapter, with real "strong answer / red flag / follow-up" breakdowns
🛠️ **A real project per chapter** — a to-do list manager, an inventory tracker, a data-cleaning toolkit, a tip-splitting calculator library, an expense tracker — not another "print the Fibonacci sequence" toy

No sign-up. No paywall. No "enter your email to unlock module 2." Just clone it, or open the link, and start.
