## LinkedIn Post

A few months ago, I was exactly where a lot of you are right now.

Reading about GenAI everywhere. Excited by it. And a little overwhelmed by it — bootcamps that cost more than a month's rent, YouTube tutorials that stop right when things get interesting, and a nagging feeling that I was falling behind on something I hadn't even properly started.

So I did the only thing that felt right: I built the course I wished existed. And then I decided nobody else should have to pay for it.

Today I'm giving it away. All of it. 🎁

---

**What "GenAI for Everyone" actually is**

Not a landing page. Not a waitlist. A complete, working, 7-week curriculum — 42 real sessions — sitting in a public GitHub repo right now, free for anyone to clone, run, and learn from.

📍 **Week 1 —** Foundations: what GenAI actually is, how LLMs work, without the math fear
📍 **Week 2 —** Prompt engineering that goes beyond "write me a poem"
📍 **Week 3 —** Embeddings & RAG — the #1 real-world GenAI pattern in industry
📍 **Week 4 —** AI agents & tool use, built from scratch, not from a black-box library
📍 **Week 5 —** Evaluation & safety — the part that separates a toy from something you'd trust in production
📍 **Week 6 —** Deployment, cost engineering, and the MLOps most courses skip entirely
📍 **Week 7 —** A real capstone, mock interviews, and a portfolio you can actually show someone

Every single session has a lesson doc, hands-on Python code, a quiz, and both a "core path" and a harder "pro path" — because beginners and working professionals deserve to learn side by side, not in separate, dumbed-down tracks.

---

**Why I'm doing this for free**

Because the person this is for is the person I was a few months ago.

The career-switcher doing this at 11pm after a full day of work. The student who can't justify a $2,000 bootcamp on a gamble. The professional who knows AI matters for their career but has nowhere honest to start that isn't a 5-minute "hello world" demo.

Nobody should need a corporate training budget to learn how to actually build with GenAI.

---

**This wasn't quick, and I won't pretend it was**

This is 42 sessions of real content — not filler. Working code you can run today, for free, with an open-source path built into every single exercise. Full written exams. Interview-prep question banks with real "what a strong answer sounds like" breakdowns, because getting hired is the actual point, not just finishing a course.

I built it because I was learning it. And I'm sharing it because I remember exactly how much it would have meant to find something like this for free.

---

**If this is you — take it. Fork it. Use it for your own study group. Build your capstone. Land the interview.**
